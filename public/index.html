The Long and Storied Life of Jose Montoya

A novel by David Dewhirst

Caught in the middle of a battle in 1915 revolutionary Mexico at the age of 15, Jose Montoya meets La Catrina, the Fancy Lady of Death. Thus begins a life-long love affair that can never be consummated and that fills Jose's life with spirits and magic from the deserts of Mexico to Ohio and beyond.
